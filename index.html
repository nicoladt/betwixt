Betwixt

A series of musical experiments


Betwixt was launched in February 2017 as an experimental, venue-independent, new music performance series.

Co-founded by Cara Stacey and Nicola du Toit, it aims to create a platform for musicians of all genres to experiment and perform in a supportive and inclusive environment. As curators, we strongly encourage and faciliate collaboration between our performers. We also actively seek to host performances in new and unusual spaces - from intimate house concerts to industrial workshops.

Betwixt brings together musicians and audiences in a new format, in an attempt to grow a community of experimental performing arts, and to create a safe space for dialogue, conversation, connectivity for performers and audiences alike. The project aims to do all of this in an inclusive, accessible and sustainable way, that actively takes into consideration South Africa's geo-spatial and socio-economic divisions and complexities.

The format is generally three short sets of new music, in diverse venues in Johannesburg, Cape Town and beyond.
